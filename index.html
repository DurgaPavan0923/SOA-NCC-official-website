About Us


About SOA NCC

Established as an integral part of the prestigious 4 (O) CTC and 1 (O) BN under the Odisha Directorate, SOA NCC Unit stands as a beacon of excellence in character development, leadership training, and national service.

Affiliated with Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University, we are committed to nurturing young minds into disciplined citizens and future leaders who embody the highest ideals of patriotism, integrity, and service.

Core Principles

NCC Objectives

Building character, developing leadership, and fostering national unity through disciplined training and service

Aim

  • Develop character, comradeship, discipline, and leadership
  • Create a human resource of organized, trained youth
  • Provide environment to motivate young people

Vision

  • Empower volunteer youth to become leaders
  • Develop leadership and character building
  • Create disciplined and responsible citizens

Motto

  • Unity and Discipline as core values
  • National Integration and secular outlook
  • Spirit of adventure and sportsmanship

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about joining NCC

Why should I join NCC?

NCC develops leadership skills, builds character, provides adventure opportunities, and offers career advantages in defense and government services. It's a platform for personal growth and national service.

What are the benefits of NCC?

Benefits include leadership development, adventure activities, career opportunities in defense services, personality development, national integration, and lifelong friendships with like-minded individuals.

Is there any fee for joining NCC?

No, there is no fee for joining NCC. The program is completely free, and cadets receive uniforms, training, and participate in camps without any cost.

What is the selection process?

Selection is based on academic performance, physical fitness, and interview. Interested students need to apply during the admission process and clear the basic fitness and interview rounds.

What events and activities are conducted?

We conduct regular parades, adventure camps, community service activities, blood donation drives, tree plantation, cultural programs, and participate in national-level competitions and camps.
